Battle Rules
  • Your normal attacks will always hit, but will deal 75% of your attack.
  • Your strong attacks will deal 100% of your attack, but have a 25% chance to miss.
  • Your evade chance is based on your speed; successful evades will heal you by 100% of your speed.
  • Defending will mitigate some damage and reflect back the rest. The mitigated damage is rng based on your defense stat.
  • The opponent has a chance to deal a strong attack, dealing twice their attack damage than normal. Pay attention to the dialogue box for this!
